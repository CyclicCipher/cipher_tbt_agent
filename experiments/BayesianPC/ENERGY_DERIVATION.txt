Energy Computation Derivation for BPC

From Equation 5:
E_l = 0.5 * <(z - Wf(z_{l-1}))^T Σ^{-1} (z - Wf(z_{l-1}))>_{q(W,Σ)}

Expanding the quadratic:
= 0.5 * E[z^T Σ^{-1} z - 2z^T Σ^{-1} Wf + f^T W^T Σ^{-1} W f]
= 0.5 * (z^T E[Σ^{-1}] z - 2z^T E[Σ^{-1}W] f + f^T E[W^T Σ^{-1} W] f)

From Equations 17-18 (page 9):
<Σ^{-1}W>_{q(W,Σ)} = ν Ψ M
<W^T Σ^{-1} W>_{q(W,Σ)} = M^T ν Ψ M + d_l V

Substituting:
= 0.5 * (z^T (νΨ) z - 2z^T (νΨM) f + f^T (M^T νΨ M + d_l V) f)

Rearranging:
= 0.5 * ((z - Mf)^T (νΨ) (z - Mf) + f^T (d_l V) f)

So the energy has TWO terms:
1. Precision-weighted prediction error: 0.5 * (z - Mf)^T (νΨ) (z - Mf)
2. Weight uncertainty penalty: 0.5 * f^T (d_l V) f

For a BATCH of n samples:
E_l = Σ_n [0.5 * (z_n - Mf_n)^T (νΨ) (z_n - Mf_n) + 0.5 * f_n^T (d_l V) f_n]

NOT:
E_l = ... + 0.5 * d_l * Tr(V) * (Σ_n ||f_n||^2)  ← WRONG (what I implemented)

The second term should be INSIDE the sum over samples, and it's f^T (d_l V) f,
not d_l * Tr(V) * ||f||^2
